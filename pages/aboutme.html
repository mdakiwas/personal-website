Hello! I am Marlette Jessa Dakiwas, a Filipino-American student attending the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am a senior studying Information Science and New Media Communication, with a minor in Chinese. I am a Carolina Covenant, Road to Hire, and Project One scholar.

Outside the classroom, I am a Design Executive of UNC-CH Southeast Asian Student Association and a two-year Gaming Assistant under UNC-CH's Gaming Arena. I volunteer in the Carolina Covenant office as a Covenant Connector, ensuring to connect fellow Covenant scholars to valued campus and program resources.

Additionally, I am a Road to Hire Campus Student Ambassador tasked with assisting in the smooth transition into college of UNC-CH students under the Road to Hire Program. My roots as an immigrant, first-generation, and Title-1 student has impassioned me to advocate for student equity and in helping students like myself in achieving their best selves in college and beyond.

Outside of academics, I am a family and friends-oriented person and love to hangout with my family and friends! Some hobbies I enjoy are going to the gym, drawing, crocheting, and reading. I'm every much a home-body and enjoy the indoors, but I love to hike through nature trails and go to scenic landscapes.
